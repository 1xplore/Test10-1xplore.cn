01 · 我们是谁 01 · Who

我们是一支从数据源头出发的 AI 团队 An AI team, starting at the data source.

一探（1xplore）成立于对"数据真相"的执念。我们将模型和中台视为治理的起点，探寻数据源头，盘活数据的全生命周期，为企业降本增效。 1xplore was founded on a stubborn belief about data. We treat models and platforms as the starting point of governance — tracing the data source, activating the entire data lifecycle, and helping enterprises reduce cost and increase efficiency.

我们是一支小团队，没有大厂光环，也不追逐风口。但我们交付过的每一个项目，都用点对点的方式跟进到能跑起来为止。我们相信，慢一点、扎实一点，反而更快。 We are a small team — no big-tech halo, no hype-chasing. Every project we take on, we follow through point-by-point until it actually runs. Slower and steadier, we believe, is the faster way.
